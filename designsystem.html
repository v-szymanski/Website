REVVITY INTERNSHIP

Building a Custom Design System for Medical Applications

Overview

This project is an ongoing effort at Revvity to establish a single source of truth for all product design, including software, hardware, and reagents. Initiated in 2022 during my first internship, I helped evolve the design system into a scalable and company-wide project involving the design team, senior leaders, developers, and product managers. I played a key role in launching the project and designed various components, from small 'atoms' like buttons and text fields to larger 'systems' like log-in and system status. The public documentation website is available at revvity.design.
